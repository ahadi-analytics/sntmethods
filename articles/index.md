### Get started

- [Get
  started](https://ahadi-analytics.github.io/sntmethods/articles/getting-started.md):

  A short tour of what sntmethods does, how the three workflows fit
  together, and how to install it - including the optional
  spatial-modeling stack.

### Workflows

- [DHS survey
  analysis](https://ahadi-analytics.github.io/sntmethods/articles/dhs-survey-analysis.md):

  Discover surveys, inspect the available DHS variables, then compute
  survey-weighted indicators across 16 domains with confidence intervals
  and admin stratification.

- [Spatial modeling
  (MBG)](https://ahadi-analytics.github.io/sntmethods/articles/spatial-modeling.md):

  Turn cluster-level survey data into continuous raster surfaces and
  population-weighted admin estimates with model-based geostatistics.

- [Routine data: incidence &
  TPR](https://ahadi-analytics.github.io/sntmethods/articles/routine-incidence.md):

  Estimate malaria incidence from health-facility data with the N0-N5
  cascade, compute test positivity with structured fallbacks, and
  explore the result.

- [Trend
  analysis](https://ahadi-analytics.github.io/sntmethods/articles/trend-analysis.md):

  Decompose monthly time series and test for direction and significance
  with STL, Mann-Kendall, and Sen’s slope.
